About the Author

Brian M. Shoemaker

Brian M. Shoemaker is a fantasy author whose debut novel, "Doraleous and Associates: A Tale of Glory," brings readers into a rich world of adventure and magic. Drawing inspiration from classic fantasy literature and mythology, Brian creates compelling characters and immersive storylines that blend action with meaningful character development.

With a passion for storytelling that began in childhood, Brian has spent years crafting the world of Doraleous and his companions. When not writing, Brian enjoys exploring nature, studying mythology, and connecting with fellow fantasy enthusiasts.
