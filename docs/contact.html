Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request a book signing event?

Contact us using the speaking engagements email address or the contact form above. Please include your location, preferred dates, expected attendance, and event details. We typically require 4-6 weeks advance notice for scheduling.

Do you accept unsolicited manuscripts or book ideas?

While Brian appreciates the creativity of fellow writers, he does not accept unsolicited manuscripts, story ideas, or collaboration proposals from unpublished authors. For published authors or industry professionals, please use the media contact email.

Can I interview Brian for my publication or podcast?

Yes! Brian enjoys discussing his work and the fantasy genre. Please contact us through the media requests email with details about your publication, audience, and interview format. Include your media kit or publication information.

How do I invite Brian to speak at my book club?

Book club visits are one of Brian's favorite activities. Contact us with your club's meeting details, preferred format (in-person or virtual), and any specific topics you'd like to discuss. Virtual visits are available for clubs anywhere in the world.

When will the next book in the series be released?

The second book in the Doraleous series is currently in development with an expected release in Spring 2025. Subscribe to the newsletter for the latest updates on publication dates and exclusive previews.

Do you offer personalized book dedications?

Yes! For special occasions or gifts, Brian can provide personalized dedications. Please contact us with your request, including the recipient's name and any specific message you'd like included.
