Doraleous and Associates: A Tale of Glory

An Epic Fantasy Adventure

Genre: Epic Fantasy
Pages: 456
Published: 2024
Format: Hardcover, Paperback, eBook, Audiobook

In a world where magic flows through ancient lands and legendary creatures roam the wilderness, Doraleous leads a band of skilled mercenaries on their most dangerous quest yet. When a mysterious client offers them a fortune to retrieve a powerful artifact from the depths of the Shadowlands, the team must navigate treacherous political intrigue, face mythical beasts, and confront their own demons.

As alliances shift and enemies emerge from unexpected quarters, Doraleous and his associates discover that their mission holds the key to preventing an ancient evil from consuming the realm. With wit, courage, and unbreakable bonds of friendship, they must rise to become the heroes their world desperately needs.

Brian M. Shoemaker weaves a tale of adventure, magic, and heroism that will captivate readers from the first page to the last. Rich with detailed world-building, complex characters, and pulse-pounding action, this novel establishes the foundation for an epic fantasy series that promises to become a modern classic.

The Doraleous Universe

A Rich and Expanding World

The world of Doraleous and Associates represents the beginning of an extensive fantasy universe, carefully crafted with intricate political systems, diverse cultures, and a magic system that operates under consistent, logical rules. This inaugural volume introduces readers to the core characters and establishes the foundational mythology that will drive future adventures.

Brian M. Shoemaker has developed a comprehensive timeline spanning thousands of years, detailed maps of multiple continents, and complex relationships between various kingdoms and magical entities. Each book in the planned series will explore different aspects of this world while maintaining the character-driven storytelling that makes the adventures personally meaningful.
