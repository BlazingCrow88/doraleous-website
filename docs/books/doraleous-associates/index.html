About the Book

In the mystical realm of Aethermoor, where ancient magics weave through the very fabric of reality, a legendary band of mercenaries known as Doraleous and Associates has built a reputation for taking on the impossible. Led by the enigmatic and brilliant tactician Doraleous, this diverse group of warriors, scholars, and rogues operates by a strict code of honor while navigating the complex political landscape of warring kingdoms and hidden magical societies.

When a mysterious benefactor approaches them with an offer too lucrative to refuseâ€”retrieve the lost Crown of Shadows from the cursed Shadowlandsâ€”the team knows they're walking into something far more dangerous than a simple treasure hunt. The Crown, lost for over a thousand years, is said to contain the power to control the ancient shadow magic that once nearly destroyed the world.

As Doraleous and his associates venture into territories where reality itself becomes unstable, they discover that their mission is inextricably linked to a prophecy that speaks of either salvation or destruction for all the realms. Old alliances crumble, unexpected enemies emerge from the shadows, and the team must confront not only external threats but their own personal demons and hidden pasts.

Brian M. Shoemaker weaves a masterful tale that combines heart-pounding action with deep character development, creating a fantasy epic that will resonate with readers long after the final page. With its richly detailed world-building, complex magic system, and unforgettable characters, "Doraleous and Associates: A Tale of Glory" establishes the foundation for what promises to be one of fantasy literature's most compelling new series.

The World of Aethermoor

Aethermoor is a realm where magic is as natural as breathing, yet as dangerous as wildfire. Five great kingdoms maintain an uneasy peace, while ancient powers stir in forgotten corners of the world. The Shadowlands, once the seat of a mighty empire, now serve as a cursed wasteland where reality itself bends and breaks.

The magic system of Aethermoor operates on the principle of elemental harmony and personal will. Practitioners must not only master the technical aspects of spellcasting but also maintain balance within themselves and with the natural world. This creates a magic system with meaningful limitations and consequences.

The Five Kingdoms

Each kingdom represents a different approach to magic and governance, from the scholarly Halls of Lumina to the warrior clans of Ironhold.

Ancient Magic

Powerful artifacts and forgotten spells from the Age of Shadows still influence the modern world, often in unexpected ways.

The Shadowlands

A cursed realm where shadow magic runs wild, twisting the landscape and creating challenges that test both mind and spirit.

A Note from the Author

Writing "Doraleous and Associates: A Tale of Glory" has been a journey of discovery that spans over five years. What began as a simple story about a group of mercenaries evolved into something much deeperâ€”an exploration of friendship, loyalty, and the choices that define us when everything is on the line.

I've always been fascinated by stories where the bonds between characters are as important as the external conflicts they face. In creating Doraleous and his team, I wanted to explore how people from vastly different backgrounds can come together to form something greater than the sum of their parts. Each character brings their own strengths, weaknesses, and personal demons to the group, and it's in navigating these relationships that they truly grow.

The world of Aethermoor was built to support these character-driven stories while providing enough depth and mystery to sustain a long series. I hope readers will find themselves as invested in this world and these characters as I've become over the years of crafting their adventures.
